Government Use of AI

The federal government is taking bold action to use AI responsibly to address the nation’s biggest challenges while managing the technology’s risks.

Learn more about policy for government use of AI in:

Non-National Security National Security

Advancing Governance, Innovation, and Risk Management for Agency Use of AI

The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) has issued guidance to federal agencies to strengthen the effective and appropriate use of AI, advance AI innovation, and manage risks from AI in the federal government.

OMB released guidance requiring agencies to align their contracts with OMB’s policy on government use of AI, while also promoting a competitive AI market through innovative acquisition.

Read the Policy Fact Sheet Read Acquisition Policy

Framework to Advance AI Governance and Risk Management in National Security

The National Security Council has issued guidance to federal agencies using AI in national security and defense contexts to mitigate potential risks from AI and ensure that AI use aligns with our country’s core values.

Read the Policy

The federal government is leveraging AI to better serve the public across a wide array of use cases, including in healthcare, transportation, the environment, and benefits delivery.
